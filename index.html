NEW BRONX DEVELOPMENTS

Recently new luxury housing spark interest in conversation about Gentrification

Bridgeline Mott Haven Bronx, NY

New kind of upscale city living sits in the South Bronx, leased and market by Citi Habitats New Development.

The completed apartments holds 91 units and is approximately 12 stories with a 6 floorplan. It includes amenities of a bike room, virtual doorman and a roof deck. Residents also get to enjoy in unit washers and dryers.

The Crescendo Mott Haven Bronx, NY

A reimagined living expereince. Composed of two buildings, "The Staccato" and "The Legato." Also known as a gradual increase; a crescendo of excitement. Creating unity between the buildings through piano rhythms and feature shareable community amentities.

Designed by Karl Fishcer and built in 2017, the building is the first complex to combine multiple lifestyle elements. The six-story building has 130 units with a starting price of $2,320 for 1 bedrroom including a balcony, living/dinning room and bathroom.

Riverdale Parc Riverdale Bronx, NY

Urban chic lifestyle residental experience feauting modern amenities including indoor- outdoor cafe, panoramic views, front/desk lobby concierge and much more.

Exquisite community of 54 studios, one, two and three bedroom residences for rent. The 7 story building is just minutes away from Manhattan and is accesible by bus and train.

The Capri Grand Concourse Bronx, NY

A 24-hour doorman building on the Grand Concourse standing sixteen stories tall. Includes a furnished lobby with contemporary details along with a fitness center. Accessible to the D and B lines.

Originally built in 1963, the building holds 158 units. The architectually building also known as "Park Avenue of The Bronx" is surrounded by attractions like the Yankee Stadium and Bronx Musuem of The Arts.
